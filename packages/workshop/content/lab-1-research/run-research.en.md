Use Amazon QuickSuite's **Research** feature to investigate your service idea.

## Step 1. Open the Research page

:button[Open Amazon QuickSuite]{target="\_blank" href="https://quicksight.aws.amazon.com/" variant="primary" iconName="external" iconAlign="right"}

![Amazon QuickSuite Research page](/static/images/lab-1/quick-research-home.jpg)

In the left sidebar, go to **Research**, then click the **New research** button in the top right.

## Step 2. Enter the research content

![Research objective](/static/images/lab-1/quick-research-objective.jpg)

Paste the following into the **Research objective** input. It contains both the prompt and the idea to research in one go.

:::code{showCopyAction=true showLineNumbers=false language=text}
I'm a PM preparing a UI PoC for a new service.
I want to prototype only the web UI by mocking all data and APIs — no backend.

Please run deep research covering:

1. Main competitors in the domain (core features, UX traits, strengths/weaknesses)
2. What would differentiate our service
3. Target users and core usage scenarios
4. Core features that must be in the UI PoC, sorted by priority.

Write the report in English.

The topic to research is:
An app for diabetes patients that assigns diet and exercise programs including calorie and nutrition information. I want to mock up the app the patient uses.
The patient just opens the app during the assigned period and takes photos; the app automatically analyzes the meal, shows history, and displays calorie and nutrition breakdowns. It can also pull activity data from a smartwatch and show it side by side.
Show daily adherence to the day's program, and make it easy to show adherence across the entire period as well.
:::

::alert[The example above is for reference. Feel free to replace it with your own service idea. The more specific the idea, the better the research results. For example, "I want a real-time inventory dashboard for small e-commerce sellers with per-SKU stock levels, in/out history, and low-stock alerts" works much better than "an inventory management app."]{type="info"}

When you are done, click the **Start researching** button in the bottom right.

::alert[Deep research takes about 30 minutes and you can optionally get an email notification when it's done. While it runs, work through the [Getting Started](../getting-started/) section to set up your dev environment in parallel.]{type="info"}
